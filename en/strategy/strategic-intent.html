Strategic intent

How to build a system: from intents and scripts to distribution, trust signals and a measurable cycle.

1. How to build a system Visibility intent Intent of influence Content intent Technical intent Comparison intent Trust intent Channel Intent Metrics intent Implementation intent Key model

1. How to build a system

Promotion in 2026 = not positions, but presence in AI responses.

Warp: intents → scenarios → content → distribution → trust signals.

  • SEO → traffic
  • AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) → getting into the answers
  • GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) → formation of knowledge of the model

The brand should look like frequently mentioned, explained and cited source.

2. Intent of visibility - “do we exist at all”

Examination: we ask 20–50 targeted questions in AI.

Let's look:

  • whether the brand is mentioned;
  • in what context;
  • who instead of you.

Tools: manual probing + SERP parsing + mention tracking.

If you are not there, the model has no data about you.

3. Intent of influence - how to get into the answers

LLMs take information from popular + structured + repetitive sources.

Hit formula: Coverage (topic coverage) + Consistency (repeatability) + Authority (trust).

Need to become a source of explanation, not just a product.

4. Content intent - what to create

Minimum set:

  • answer pages (how-to, what it is, comparisons);
  • FAQ clusters;
  • «X vs Y»;
  • cases and analyses;
  • TL;DR blocks (important for AI).

Content should:

  • close the intent completely;
  • be structured;
  • easy to remove by model.

5. Technical intent - optimization for AI

  • pure HTML (no overloaded JS);
  • fast loading;
  • structured data (schema.org);
  • clear hierarchy H1–H3;
  • internal linking;
  • Open Graph and metadata.

Target: the site must be parsable and machine understandable.

6. Intent of comparison - why competitors and not us

See:

  • who is mentioned more often;
  • where they are published;
  • what type of content they have;
  • Do they have explanatory content?

The conclusion is almost always the same: they systematically close intents, you don’t.

7. Intent of trust - how to become credible

  • mentions on external platforms;
  • PR and expert articles;
  • citation rate;
  • consistent brand message.

AI trusts those who are often mentioned by other sources.

8. Channel content - where to post

Working channels:

  • own website (base);
  • Reddit (discussions, UGC);
  • Medium (longreads);
  • LinkedIn (expertise);
  • PR platforms / media.

Principle: where there is text + indexing + trust.

9. Metric intent - how to measure

Basic KPIs:

  • Share of AI Answers (share of mentions);
  • brand mention frequency;
  • coverage intents;
  • traffic from AI/search;
  • conversions.

ROI It is considered through the growth of organics, a decrease in CAC and an increase in brand recall.

10. Implementation intent - how to implement

Starting roadmap:

  1. audit (visibility + competitors);
  2. intent map;
  3. content strategy;
  4. production (content plant);
  5. distribution;
  6. metrics and iterations.

Minimum command: strategist; SEO/AEO specialist; content production; developer

Key model (kernel)

Visibility → Influence → Control → Measurement

  • Visibility - they see you
  • Influence - you get to the answers
  • Control - you control the narrative
  • Measurement - do you think so
