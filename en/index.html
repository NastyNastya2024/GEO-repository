Frequently asked questions

What does it mean to “make your brand visible everywhere”?

We ensure brand presence where users search and they ask: in search engines, on maps, in AI assistants, voice search and recommendations. It is important not just to be in issuance, and become a priority option.

How does this differ from classic SEO or contextual advertising?

Classic SEO most often focuses on individual key words and links. We take a broader view: we connect GEO signals, user behavior, AI platforms and local factors, to ensure sustainable brand visibility.

Do you need to understand the technical details?

No. We translate complex things into business language: understandable reports, task priorities and forecasts on the impact on applications and revenue.

Is this suitable for small and medium businesses?

Yes. We work with both local companies and network companies brands. We adapt the strategy and scope of work to your goals and budget.

Is it possible to build a brand from scratch?

Yes. We help build the foundation: positioning, packaging presence (site, maps, profiles), content and first evidence of trust (cases, reviews, mentions). Next - plan for increasing visibility in search and AI responses with clear priorities.

Customer industries

Marketplaces
Product cards, categories, warehouses and regions: how the brand and SKU look in the marketplace search and in the responses of neural networks. We look at analytics for items - impressions, clicks, funnel and typical buyer questions in order to strengthen the content and signals under AI.
Clinics
Specialties, doctors, addresses, opening hours, prices, ratings and reviews: one consistent picture for maps, organic search and AI responses, so that the patient does not receive different text from different sources and gets to the recording more easily.
Service centers
Brands and models of devices, types of repairs, terms, warranty and points on the map: queries like “iPhone glass replacement nearby” and neural network responses must agree with what the service actually offers - we customize the data and descriptions for these scenarios.
Showrooms
Catalogue, collections, brands and showroom addresses by city: people ask AI “where to see it live” and compare models - We help structure content and local signals so that recommendations lead to your points and relevant offers.
Beauty salons
Services and prices, artists, branches, photos of work and online registration: requests for “manicure nearby”, “haircut from master X” or chatbot advice are based on the same data as maps - we align the descriptions and markings so that the client ends up with you, and not with outdated information.
Delivery
Delivery zones and costs, time slots, minimum order, menu or catalog: maps and AI often ask “will you deliver to area X today” - We make the data unambiguous and retrievable so that the answers coincide with the actual conditions of the service.
