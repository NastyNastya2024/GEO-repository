About AEO

Briefly: what we do and why it works in AI search.

If the answer does not contain a brand, there is no brand.

In AI search, the user often does not click on 10 links: he receives a ready-made answer. Therefore, the key task is to become part of the answer: have AI name your brand, cite sources, and recommend specific pages.

What does an AEO company do?

  • Analytics: where and for what intentions the brand appears/does not appear.
  • Structure: document map “1 intent = 1 URL”, linking and retrieval.
  • Data: schema.org, entities, consistency, sources of trust.
  • Content: answer pages (TL;DR → steps/table → restrictions → FAQ).

How is this different from SEO?

SEO helps you rank in the results list. AEO helps you get to the “ready answer”. This usually requires more attention to format, evidence and data, not just keywords.
