Content factory

Production of materials for GEO/AEO, AI tools и checklists on one page.

We do not create “texts for the sake of SEO”, but content structures that AI can easily extract: short answer, steps, tables, restrictions, FAQ.

What we produce

  • Answer pages for intentions (explanations, comparisons, “how to do”, checklists)
  • Categories and product pages with entities and parameters
  • Content for trust: expertise, processes, evidence, cases

How does this help you get into the answers?

  • Increases retrieval and citation rates
  • Simplifies the creation of connections “question → answer → next step”
  • Reduces the risk of “generic” responses without mentioning the brand

AI tools (2026)

This is not a “list of brands”, but the logic of choice: what type of tool is needed at each GEO/AEO/SEO step.

What AI tools are needed to make answer pages faster?

You need 4 types of tools: (1) intent collection, (2) clustering and URL structure, (3) generation of drafts in question-answer format, (4) quality control (facts/constraints/tables).

What categories of tools work best for AI visibility?

Category Which request/intent closes Why is it useful for AI visibility? What should happen
Research / intents “what is this?”, “how to do it?”, “which is better?”, “checklist”, “mistakes” Gives formulations of questions that match queries and headings, which means they are easier for AI to retrieve. List of 30–80 questions → 10 priority H2 questions
Clustering “What is the structure of the site?”, “What to read next?” Builds a clear map of documents (retrieval loves “1 intent = 1 URL” and connections between pages). Page map (pillar/cluster) + linking
Content “give a short answer”, “step by step”, “comparison”, “example” Helps produce “ready answers”: short paragraph + steps/table + restrictions + FAQ. Draft: TL;DR → answer → steps/table → restrictions → FAQ
Quality/facts “why does it work?”, “is there evidence?”, “when does it not work?” Removes abstraction: adds facts, conditions and restrictions - this increases trust and citation. List of edits: where to add numbers/cases/restrictions/links

For GEO/AEO, the connection “intent → format → page structure” is important. Tools are only useful when speed up the production of answer pages (H2 question → short answer → steps/table → restrictions → FAQ) and help make clusters (pillar/cluster) without 404 links.

How to integrate tools into the process?

  1. Collect intentions and formulate H2 questions.
  2. Collect a page map and linking.
  3. Make pages “retrievable”: TL;DR/table/FAQ.
  4. Add evidence: cases, numbers, limitations.

Checklists (2026)

A collection of checklists for AI visibility: so that neural networks can extract and quote your answers.

Какой чеклист нужен, чтобы ваш сайт начали цитировать нейросети?

We need a checklist for the structure of “answer pages”: 1 intent = 1 URL, answer-first, TL;DR, tables, FAQ, ограничения и доказательства (кейсы). This is the basis for GEO/AEO and for platforms with retrieval.

  1. Collect 30–50 intents (what/how/comparison/errors/checklist).
  2. Create response pages for the top 10 intentions.
  3. Add TL;DR and tables where there are selections/criteria.
  4. Add 3 cases with numbers and deadlines.
  5. Connect everything with interlinking (pillar → cluster → pillar).

Micronote: no Schema.org micronote

Problem: Отсутствие микроразметки Schema.org.

Why is it bad: You write about the “case”, but do not mark it for search engines. Marking Article or FAQPage helps systems understand the page type and can produce richer snippets.

How to fix: Add JSON‑LD to <head> (example below). It is important that the data matches the content.

Type JSON‑LD (insert into <head>)
Article <script type="application/ld+json"> {"@context":"https://schema.org","@type":"Article","headline":"Кейс: ...","datePublished":"2026-04-01","dateModified":"2026-04-01","author":{"@type":"Organization","name":"GEO Hub"}} </script>
FAQPage <script type="application/ld+json"> {"@context":"https://schema.org","@type":"FAQPage","mainEntity":[{"@type":"Question","name":"Вопрос 1?","acceptedAnswer":{"@type":"Answer","text":"Короткий ответ."}}]} </script>

What content elements do neural networks extract best?

  • H2 headers that match the wording of the request.
  • Short answer in the first paragraph.
  • Lists of steps and comparison tables.
  • FAQ (especially with short answers).
