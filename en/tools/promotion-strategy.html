Drawing up a promotion strategy

Strategy is a blueprint for decisions: which scripts to take first, which pages/entities to release, and which trust signals to strengthen.

What does it consist of?

  • Priority intents and scenarios (what AI should “know” about the brand)
  • Map of pages and content formats (answers, comparisons, reference books, cases)
  • Trust Signals and Citation Sources
  • Metrics: visibility, citations, applications, response rate

What do you get

  • Roadmap for 4–8 weeks
  • Terms of reference for content/data/technical corrections
  • Tracking and regular update plan
