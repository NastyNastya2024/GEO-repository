Who are we

We are the team that leads the project comprehensive digital promotion: organic search and website structure (SEO), visibility in neural network and AI search responses (GEO, AEO, AIO), brand consistency in social networks and public channels (AI+SMM) and related analytics. The website is our public expert base: guides, checklists and analyzes help you navigate the topic; in parallel we provide consultations and turnkey services in these areas - for companies that need practical help, not just theory.

Legal entity for contracts and accounts: IP Komarova. Brand and visual presentation on the website - GEO Hub (digital promotion and AI visibility); The name of the individual entrepreneur may appear in documents and contacts.

Services

We work with businesses that need sustainable demand channels: search, locality, AI responses and a consistent brand narrative. Typical set of directions:

  • Audit and analytics — SEO and technical base, visibility in AI responses (prompts, quoted URLs, competitors), if necessary, audit of code and data for AEO, GEO and AIO.
  • Strategy and roadmap — priorities by clusters, locality, answer pages, social networks and evidence base (cases, figures, FAQ).
  • Content and site structure — pages for search and “answer systems”, comparisons, checklists; coordination of wording with social networks and official channels.
  • Escort — regular measurements of search and AI visibility, improvements and consultations after implementation.

Detailed materials and self-study tools are in the sections Analytics and audit, Content factory и Promotion methods. The methodology is described there; On this page there is only an introduction to the company and how to work with us.

Questions about the company and cooperation

Here are the answers about the order and the format of the work. Questions like “how is GEO different from SEO” and comparison of methods - in FAQ on promotion methods.

How to get started?

Write or call - briefly describe the niche, site and goal (for example, “we are not visible in ChatGPT responses to requests about ...”). We will offer a format: a one-time consultation, an audit with a report, or a project with stages - depending on the task.

Who do you usually work with?

With companies that already have a website and a clear product: services, e-commerce, B2B, local business, digital products. If there is no website, it makes sense to close the basic storefront first - without a stable domain it is more difficult to build a stable AI visibility.

Are the materials on the site an offer?

No. The texts of the sections are for informational purposes only and do not constitute a public offer. Conditions, terms and costs are fixed separately after discussing the problem.
