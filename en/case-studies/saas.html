SaaS and digital cases

Case templates for B2B and digital products: how to show value through facts, limitations and comparisons.

What methods are a priority and a typical strategy

For SaaS and complex B2B, they often start with AEO и AIO: a cluster of answer pages (what it is, how to implement it, comparisons, limitations) so that models can pull out a quote with numbers and conditions. Then they deepen SEO according to real demand (width and linking of clusters). GEO connect if there are offices, partners or regional product restrictions. AI+SMM and PR are useful when the same points must be reinforced in open channels as on the website - otherwise AI sees different versions of the truth.

Typical strategy: define the intentions “what / how / vs / how much / security” → collect pillar + answer pages with tables and FAQ → synchronize wording on social networks and landing pages. Summary order of methods - in review of promotion methods.

How to make a SaaS case that is cited by neural networks?

Make a clear structure: problem → hypothesis → what was implemented → result in numbers → limitations → “what to repeat.”

Which pages give the most growth?

  • “X vs Y” comparisons and selection criteria tables.
  • How-to implementation guides.
  • FAQ on security/integrations/pricing.
