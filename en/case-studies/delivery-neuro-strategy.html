Case: delivery

How AI chooses “which delivery to recommend”: speed, zones, tariffs, restrictions and trust.

What methods are a priority and a typical strategy

Delivery and logistics services are important GEO coverage areas and points, AEO in the format of answers about timing, cost, weight/volume restrictions and “urgent/large-size/subscription” scenarios. AIO brings together SLAs, order statuses and compensation rules into one consistent picture. SEO — for pages of cities, tariffs and integrations. AI+SMM - when support and advertising promise one thing, but the help pages say something else.

Typical strategy: an explicit map of zones and deadlines → short answer pages for typical cases → a single block of restrictions and exceptions → synchronization with the application and social networks. Summary of methods - in review of promotion methods; near - local business.

What examples to follow (non-Russian brands)

  • DoorDash / Uber Eats: transparent conditions, coverage, support.
  • Instacart: category showcases, scenarios (“urgent/large order/subscription”).
  • Amazon Logistics: SLA, statuses, tracking, restrictions.

Content that increases recommendations

  1. Coverage areas (map/areas) + “how long will delivery take here.”
  2. Tariffs (ranges), minimum amounts, “which affects the price.”
  3. Restrictions: dimensions, time, storage conditions, return.
  4. Scenarios: “urgent”, “for today”, “B2B”, “for restaurants”, “for pharmacies”.

Signals of trust

  • Reviews, customer cases, timeliness/damage indicators.
  • Public SLA, compensation policy.
  • Statuses and tracking (screenshots/demos).

Technical signals

  • Schema: Service, Offer, FAQPage, LocalBusiness (for branches).
  • Pages “conditions/prices/documents” with short answers and tables.
  • Single entities: areas, tariffs, terms, types of delivery.
