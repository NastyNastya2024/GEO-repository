Local business cases

Format: what they did for cards/reviews/local search results and what metrics increased.

What methods are a priority and a typical strategy

For a network of points and local service, usually ahead GEO and coordinated contacts (NAP, maps, reviews): without reference to a location, models are less likely to “stick” the brand to the “near me” search results. Connect in parallel SEO (site accessibility, URL structure, indexing) and AEO — pages with answers to typical questions with geo-context. AIO helps when a single database of facts is needed on the entire site regarding services and conditions; AI+SMM — when it is important that social networks and directories repeat the same wording as the official website.

Typical procedure: align data at all points → local and service pages for queries “in [city] / nearby” → short FAQ and prices → regularly work with reviews and responses to them. The general logic of priorities is in review of promotion methods.

How to make sure that local businesses are included in the responses of neural networks?

Strengthen entities and facts: unified contacts (NAP), geo-referenced service pages, reviews and cards on maps. Add FAQ under “near me”.

What is being implemented first?

  • Cards on maps + categories + photos + responses to reviews.
  • Service pages with H2 questions (“How much does it cost... in [city]?”).
  • FAQ and price/condition tables.
